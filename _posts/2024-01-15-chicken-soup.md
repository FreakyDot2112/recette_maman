title: "Mom's Chicken Soup"
description: "The one that cures everything. Rich, golden broth with tender chicken and vegetables."
category: "Soups & Stews"
emoji: "🍲"
date: 2024-01-15
prep_time: "20 min"
cook_time: "2 hrs"
servings: 6
difficulty: Easy
ingredients:
  - 1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs)
  - 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  - 3 celery stalks, chopped
  - 1 large onion, quartered
  - 4 garlic cloves, smashed
  - 2 bay leaves
  - Small bunch of fresh parsley
  - Salt and black pepper to taste
  - Egg noodles or rice (optional, for serving)
steps:
  - Place the whole chicken in a large pot. Cover with cold water by at least 2 inches. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  - Once boiling, skim off any foam that rises to the surface. Reduce heat to a gentle simmer.
  - Add the onion, garlic, bay leaves, and half the parsley. Season generously with salt. Simmer uncovered for 1.5 hours.
  - Remove the chicken carefully and set aside to cool. Add the carrots and celery to the broth. Simmer 20 more minutes.
  - Once the chicken is cool enough to handle, pull the meat from the bones and return it to the pot. Discard the carcass and bay leaves.
  - Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot with egg noodles or crusty bread, topped with fresh parsley.
notes: "Mom always said the secret is time — don't rush the simmer. If you have leftover roasted chicken bones in the freezer, throw those in too for deeper flavor. This soup freezes beautifully for up to 3 months."
